THE SERIES

Three Arcs. One Destiny.

01
Soul Society Arc

The Soul Reaper Chronicles

Ichigo Kurosaki absorbs the powers of Soul Reaper Rukia Kuchiki to defend his family from a Hollow. Branded a criminal, Rukia is taken to Soul Society to face execution. Ichigo storms Seireitei — home of the Gotei 13 — in a desperate rescue that uncovers a conspiracy orchestrated from within.

Key revelation: Aizen fakes his death, betrays Soul Society, and steals the Hōgyoku.

02
Arrancar Arc

Hueco Mundo & the Espada

Aizen forges an army of Arrancars — Hollows who ripped off their own masks to gain Shinigami-like power. His ten elite Espada assault the living world and kidnap Orihime. Ichigo pursues them into a vast, moonlit desert realm for a brutal gauntlet before the showdown at Fake Karakura Town.

Peak: Ichigo's Hollowification and Ulquiorra's Segunda Etapa — the only Espada with a second Resurreccion form.

03
Thousand-Year Blood War

The Quincy Empire

Yhwach — Father of Quincy — leads the hidden empire of Wandenreich in a devastating invasion. His elite Sternritters bear lettered Schrift powers, slaughtering Captains and stealing Bankai. Ichigo must unlock his true Zanpakutō and full lineage to face a god who rewrites the future itself.

Truth: Ichigo carries all three bloodlines — Shinigami, Hollow, and Quincy — the pivot of a thousand-year war.

Ichigo Kurosaki

一 護 · 黒 崎

Born to a Shinigami father and a Quincy mother who was once a Hollow, Ichigo is the only being to carry all three spiritual bloodlines. This makes him the pivot point of the entire thousand-year conflict between Soul Society and the Wandenreich.

His Zanpakutō, Zangetsu, manifests as two blades — the merged truth of his Shinigami and Hollow powers — only revealed after he discovers the full nature of his heritage in the final arc.
