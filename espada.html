OVERVIEW

Arrancars of Las Noches

The Espada (十刃, Jūssantai; literally "Ten Blades") are the ten most powerful Arrancar in Sōsuke Aizen's army, residing within the vast fortress of Las Noches in Hueco Mundo. Each Espada is numbered 0–9, with the number tattooed somewhere on their body indicating their relative power — though even the weakest can overpower most Shinigami Captains.

Arrancar are Hollows who have removed their Hollow mask, gaining a human form and Shinigami-like powers while retaining their Hollow core. In Resurreccion — their released form — they shed their human guise entirely and reveal the full devastation of their original Hollow nature.
