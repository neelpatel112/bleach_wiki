OVERVIEW

Arrancars of Las Noches

The Espada (十刃) are the ten most powerful Arrancar in Sōsuke Aizen's army, residing within the fortress of Las Noches in Hueco Mundo. Each is numbered 0–9, tattooed somewhere on their body, indicating their rank of power. Even the weakest can overpower most Shinigami Captains.

Arrancar are Hollows who removed their Hollow mask, gaining a human form and Shinigami-like powers. In Resurreccion — their released form — they shed their human guise and reveal the full devastation of their original Hollow nature.

十 刃

Jūssantai · Ten Blades

ESPADA ROSTER

The Ten Blades

Overlord
Leader of Hueco Mundo

Sōsuke Aizen

Former 5th Division Captain · Orchestrator of All

Resurreccion Transcendence (Hogyoku)

Kyōka Suigetsu — complete hypnosis affecting all five senses of everyone who has seen its release. After merging with the Hōgyoku, Aizen transcended both Shinigami and Hollow, becoming a being that surpassed all categories — until Ichigo's power exceeded even that.

"From the start, no one in this world has been able to stand in the heavens and look down on me."
Espada #1
Primera Espada

Coyote Stark

Primera Espada · Aspect of Death: Loneliness

Resurreccion Los Lobos

Cero Metralleta — fires an infinite volley of Cero blasts simultaneously from the wolves that Stark split from his own soul. His power was so immense that every Hollow near him died from proximity — forcing him to split his soul into Stark and Lilynette just to survive.

"I don't want to fight. I just don't want to be alone anymore."
Espada #2
Segunda Espada

Baraggan Louisenbairn

Segunda Espada · Former God-King of Hueco Mundo

Resurreccion Arrogante

Respira — the Breath of Death. A miasma that ages everything it touches to dust in seconds — flesh, bone, weapons, and Bankai alike. His aspect of death is aging itself. Even his own body deteriorates without his armor, revealing a skeletal form beneath.

"I am death itself. There is nothing in existence that does not age and crumble before me."
Espada #3
Tercera Espada

Tier Harribel

Tercera Espada · Ruler of Hueco Mundo (post-war)

Resurreccion Tiburón

Water and blood manipulation on a massive scale. In Resurreccion, she manifests a shark-themed form that turns all moisture in the environment into weapons. Her Hirviendo boils any water-based attack and redirects it. She ultimately becomes ruler of Hueco Mundo after Aizen's defeat.

"If it is for those I protect, I will fight any enemy — and I will not fall."
Espada #4
Cuarta Espada

Ulquiorra Cifer

Cuarta Espada · Aspect of Death: Nihilism

Resurreccion Murciélago — Segunda Etapa

The only Espada to achieve a true second Resurreccion — Segunda Etapa. In this form he surpasses all other Espada and claims power that can only exist at the top of Hueco Mundo. His Lanza del Relámpago destroys everything in its blast radius. His final moment is a search for what a heart actually is.

"That is what I call a heart. If you have one, it would be right here — in the place where mine should be."
Espada #5
Quinta Espada

Nnoitra Gilga

Quinta Espada · Aspect of Death: Despair

Resurreccion Santa Teresa

Transforms into a six-armed form carrying six enormous scythes simultaneously. His Hierro — the hardened skin of an Arrancar — is the hardest of any Espada, near impenetrable. His singular obsession is finding a battle where he can finally die, having grown bored of survival.

"I won't stop — not until I find a battle where someone can actually kill me."
Espada #6
Sexta Espada

Grimmjow Jaegerjaquez

Sexta Espada · Aspect of Death: Destruction

Resurreccion Pantera

Lightning-fast Sonído and savage close-range combat. In Resurreccion he transforms into a panther-like form with claws that fire Desgarrón — ten enormous claw beams. Untameable by even Aizen, his obsession with defeating Ichigo in a fair fight is his only consistent motivation.

"I am the king. And I will keep clawing my way back up — no matter how many times you knock me down."
Espada #7
Séptima Espada

Zommari Rureaux

Séptima Espada · Fastest Sonído User

Resurreccion Brujería

50 eyes of Amor bloom across his body in Resurreccion — each capable of taking over the soul of whatever limb or body part it makes contact with. Victims cannot control their own bodies. Claims to have the fastest Sonído of any Espada and an intense devotion to Aizen bordering on religious.

"You Shinigami think yourselves superior — yet you have never truly understood the meaning of speed."
Espada #8
Octava Espada

Szayelaporro Granz

Octava Espada · Scientist of Hueco Mundo

Resurreccion Fornicarás

Creates dolls from captured opponents, allowing him to destroy organs inside living enemies by breaking the doll. Can also self-replicate by consuming a host body from the inside — reincarnating himself through others. He believes he has prepared for every possible scenario. He was wrong.

"I have prepared for every possibility. There is no scenario in existence in which I can lose."
Espada #9
Novena Espada

Aaroniero Arruruerie

Novena Espada · The Only Gillian-Class Espada

Resurreccion Glotonería

Has absorbed 33,650 Hollows and all their powers — the only Vasto Lorde or Gillian-class Arrancar among the Espada. His two heads contain the souls of every Hollow consumed. By absorbing Metastacia, he also contained the memories and powers of the deceased Soul Reaper Kaien Shiba, using them to torment Rukia.

"I am every Hollow that has ever existed. I am without limit. I am endless."
