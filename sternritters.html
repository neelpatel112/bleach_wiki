The Star Cross Knights

The Sternritters (星十字騎士団, Sternritter; German for "Star Knights") are the elite Quincy warriors of the Wandenreich — the hidden empire ruled by Yhwach, the Father of the Quincy and progenitor of Ichigo's bloodline. They have lived in secret for over a thousand years, nursing their hatred of Soul Society for the genocide of their kind.

Each Sternritter is granted a Schrift (聖文字, lit. "Sacred Writing") — a letter from A to Z that defines a unique, catastrophic power. In the Wandenreich's two invasions, they systematically defeated Shinigami Captains, stole their Bankai, and pushed Soul Society to the edge of extinction.
