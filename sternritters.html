The Star Cross Knights

The Sternritters (星十字騎士団) are the elite Quincy warriors of the Wandenreich — the hidden empire ruled by Yhwach, the Father of the Quincy. Hidden for over a thousand years, nursing their hatred of Soul Society for the genocide of their kind centuries ago.

Each Sternritter is granted a Schrift — a lettered power unique to them, ranging from A to Z. In two consecutive invasions, they systematically defeated Shinigami Captains, stole their Bankai, and pushed Soul Society to total collapse.

STERNRITTER ROSTER

The Lettered Warriors

A His Majesty · Emperor
Schrift A — The Almighty

Yhwach

Emperor of the Wandenreich · Father of the Quincy

Schrift: A The Almighty

The Almighty grants foresight and the power to modify all futures he has foreseen. He can also perform Auswählen — reclaiming the powers of Quincy he deems weak. As the progenitor of all Quincy, his blood flows through Ichigo, making him a linchpin of the entire conflict's origin.

"Everything that exists in this world moves according to my will — including you."
B Grand Master
Schrift B — The Balance

Jugram Haschwalth

Grand Master of the Sternritters · Yhwach's Right Hand

Schrift: B The Balance

The Balance — any misfortune that befalls Haschwalth is automatically redistributed to his enemies. He is functionally untouchable. While Yhwach sleeps, Haschwalth holds the Almighty in his place, becoming the most powerful being in existence during those hours.

"The scales of the world must remain balanced. Enforcing that balance — that is my sole purpose."
A Yhwach's Successor
Schrift A — The Antithesis

Uryū Ishida

Yhwach's Chosen Successor · Last Quincy

Schrift: A The Antithesis

The Antithesis — reverses anything that has occurred between two designated targets. Damage, healing, status — all of it is swapped. As the sole survivor of Yhwach's Auswählen as a child, he carries a unique resistance. His true goal throughout the final arc is to destroy Yhwach from the inside.

"I walk my own path. Even if that path leads straight through the heart of my enemy."
H Sternritter
Schrift H — The Heat

Bazz-B

Sternritter H · Childhood Friend of Haschwalth

Schrift: H The Heat

The Heat — fires devastating fire attacks from his index fingers. The output scales exponentially as he takes more damage in battle. One finger can incinerate a Captain-level opponent. Five fingers simultaneously is considered his maximum — enough to destroy an entire battlefield.

"I'll burn everything to the ground — starting with you, then working my way up to him."
F Sternritter
Schrift F — The Fear

Äs Nödt

Sternritter F · Wielder of Byakuya's Bankai

Schrift: F The Fear

The Fear — injects thorns into victims' bodies that amplify the base emotion of fear beyond all rational thought. The fear produced is not emotional — it is a direct attack on the nervous system, causing complete paralysis. His Vollständig: Tatarforas reveals his true, horrific form.

"I don't create fear. I simply reveal what was always hiding there, underneath everything."
K Sternritter
Schrift K — The Killer

BG9

Sternritter K · Mechanical Quincy Warrior

Schrift: K The Killer

A mechanized body housing multiple simultaneous weapon systems — guided missiles, energy blasts, tentacle cables, and rapid-fire projectiles. Can operate multiple weapon systems simultaneously. Fought and nearly defeated Suì-Fēng, stealing classified information from Soul Society during the first invasion.

"Target acquired. Combat parameters set. Elimination commencing."
T Sternritter
Schrift T — The Thunderbolt

Candice Catnipp

Sternritter T · Lightning Goddess

Schrift: T The Thunderbolt

The Thunderbolt generates and fires a colossal lightning column capable of vaporizing anything in its path. She can absorb electricity to power and heal herself, then redirect the absorbed energy as amplified attacks. Her Vollständig gives her lightning wings and massively increases output.

"You want to mess with me? Fine — but don't come crying when I've fried every cell in your body."
I Sternritter
Schrift I — The Iron

Cang Du

Sternritter I · Wielder of Hitsugaya's Bankai

Schrift: I The Iron

The Iron — reinforces his skin to a level that deflects virtually all spiritual attacks, including Bankai strikes. Stole Hitsugaya's Daiguren Hyōrinmaru during the first invasion and used it against Soul Society's own forces. One of the first Sternritters executed by Yhwach for failing in the second invasion.

"True strength is not the blade. It is the skin that no blade can cut."
Z Sternritter
Schrift Z — The Zombie

Giselle Gewelle

Sternritter Z · Necromancer of the Wandenreich

Schrift: Z The Zombie

The Zombie — anyone who touches Giselle's blood becomes a perfectly obedient zombie under her control. This works on Shinigami just as effectively as it does on Quincy and humans. She zombified Captain Tōshirō Hitsugaya and used him as a weapon against his own allies.

"Death is just the beginning of real loyalty. The dead never disobey."
G Sternritter
Schrift G — The Glutton

Liltotto Lamperd

Sternritter G · Survivor of Yhwach's Betrayal

Schrift: G The Glutton

The Glutton — a gigantic mouth that extends from her small frame capable of consuming anything: opponents, their abilities, spiritual constructs, even the ground itself. Survived Yhwach's Auswählen along with Giselle and later allied with the Shinigami out of a desire for revenge against Yhwach himself.

"Everything looks edible when you're hungry enough — and right now, I'm starving for payback."
S Sternritter
Schrift S — The Superstar

Mask De Masculine

Sternritter S · The People's Champion

Schrift: S The Superstar

The Superstar — gains power exponentially from cheers and applause. His cheerleader James exists solely to cheer for him, and as long as James cheers, Mask becomes stronger and can even heal from fatal wounds. He defeated both Renji Abarai and Rukia Kuchiki simultaneously at full strength.

"Everyone loves a winner — and I have never, ever lost! The crowd demands a hero!"
U Sternritter
Schrift U — The Underbelly

NaNaNa Najahkoop

Sternritter U · Analyst Warrior

Schrift: U The Underbelly

The Underbelly — creates a pressure map of an opponent's spiritual energy distribution, revealing the precise gaps and weak points in their power that can be exploited. Every attack he launches targets these identified weaknesses with surgical precision, bypassing conventional defense entirely.

"Every defense has a gap. I find it in seconds — then I exploit it until there's nothing left."
C Sternritter
Schrift C — The Compulsory

Pernida Parnkgjas

Sternritter C · Left Hand of the Soul King

Schrift: C The Compulsory

The Compulsory — nerves extend outward to control and restructure the physical composition of anything they touch, bending bone, metal, and flesh alike. Can forcibly evolve its own power to overcome any ability it encounters, adapting in real-time. Its true form is the detached left hand of the Soul King itself.

"I will bend everything — your bones, your blade, and ultimately your will."
R Sternritter
Schrift R — The Rangi Range

Robert Accutrone

Sternritter R · Long-Range Precision Fighter

Schrift: R The Rangi Range

The Rangi Range — spatially compresses the effective range of his shots, allowing attacks to bypass conventional defenses through dimensional shortcutting. His bullets do not travel through normal space — they arrive. Engaged Shunsui Kyōraku during the first invasion, wounding the future Captain-Commander.

"Range is irrelevant to me. Distance is just a number — and I always hit exactly what I aim for."
