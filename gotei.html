OVERVIEW

The Court Guard Squads

The Gotei 13 (護廷十三隊) is the primary military force of Soul Society, formed over a thousand years ago by thirteen ruthless warriors led by Genryūsai Yamamoto. Evolved into a disciplined army of 13 numbered divisions, each with its own Captain, Lieutenant, and seated officers.

A Captain's requirement is absolute — mastery of Bankai, achieved by fewer than 1 in 1,000 Shinigami. Together they patrol the flow of souls between worlds, purify Hollows, and defend the balance of the universe.

Ichigo Kurosaki

Substitute Shinigami · Protagonist

Bankai Tensa Zangetsu

Getsuga Tenshō — a compressed wave of spiritual energy fired from his blade. After unlocking his true Zanpakutō, he wields two blades representing his merged Shinigami and Hollow heritage. Capable of Final Getsuga Tenshō, becoming Getsuga itself at the cost of his powers.

"If fate is a millstone, then we are the grist. There is nothing we can do."

1st Division · Captain-Commander

Genryūsai Yamamoto

Captain-Commander of the Gotei 13

Bankai Zanka no Tachi

Ryūjin Jakka — the oldest and most powerful fire-type Zanpakutō in existence. In Bankai, his blade concentrates all flames into a single point of absolute heat. Zanka no Tachi has four directional techniques — each capable of annihilating an army.

"Those who do not know what war is should not speak of peace."
2nd Division
2nd Division · Captain

Suì-Fēng

Captain of the 2nd Division · Commander of Onmitsukidō

Bankai Jakuhō Raikōben

Suzumebachi — a two-touch death weapon; hitting the same spot twice leaves a skull crest that kills instantly. Her Bankai fires a giant guided missile she despises using, as it is too large for her preference of covert assassination.

"Speed is the ultimate power. Anything that can't keep up deserves to fall."
3rd Division
3rd Division · Captain

Rōjūrō Ōtoribashi

Captain of the 3rd Division · Rose · Visored

Bankai Kinshara Butōdan

Kinshara — a golden whip that forces victims to experience any sensation its melody dictates. In Bankai, he creates a full orchestra of illusion performers who execute attacks through their music, forcing enemies to live out whatever his compositions demand.

"Music is not a language. It is the true expression of the soul."
4th Division
4th Division · Captain

Retsu Unohana

Captain of the 4th Division · First Kenpachi

Bankai Minazuki

Minazuki — a giant manta-ray-like creature that heals any wound by enveloping victims in acidic gastric fluid. Her true identity is the original Kenpachi — the most bloodthirsty and feared Shinigami in history, who sealed her power behind a healer's facade for a thousand years.

"There is no wound I cannot heal — and no swordsman alive I cannot kill."
5th Division
5th Division · Captain

Shinji Hirako

Captain of the 5th Division · Visored Leader

Bankai Sakanade

Sakanade — inverts the senses of everyone in range: up becomes down, left becomes right, front becomes back. Even knowing the inversion exists does not allow you to compensate for it. As a former Visored, he can augment this with a Hollow mask.

"You can't fight what you can't see — or what you only think you can see."
6th Division
6th Division · Captain

Byakuya Kuchiki

Captain of the 6th Division · 28th Head of Kuchiki Clan

Bankai Senbonzakura Kageyoshi

Senbonzakura — the blade shatters into a thousand tiny blades resembling cherry blossoms. In Bankai, two enormous swords dissolve into millions of razor petals that move faster than the eye can follow. Final form Shukei: Hakuteiken concentrates them all into a single transcendent blade.

"Pride is not merely a shield — it is the very sword that guards everything worth protecting."
7th Division
7th Division · Captain

Sajin Komamura

Captain of the 7th Division · Wolfman Warrior

Bankai Kokujō Tengen Myō'ō

Tenken — summons giant body parts that mirror Komamura's own movements. Bankai manifests a colossal armored warrior that mirrors every attack at enormous scale. His ultimate technique, the Humanization Technique, turns him fully human — gaining infinite healing power at the cost of becoming mortal and surrendering his soul.

"I will fight to protect what matters — even if it costs me my immortality."
8th Division
8th Division · Captain-Commander (later)

Shunsui Kyōraku

Captain of the 8th Division · Second Captain-Commander

Bankai Katen Kyōkotsu: Karamatsu Shinjū

Katen Kyōkotsu — twin blades whose games become reality; all within range must obey the rules. In Bankai, he traps the opponent in an intimate space where four sequential games of death play out, each more devastating than the last. The final stage is a death duet — they share all wounds.

"In this game, we must see it through to the very end — even if we don't like how it finishes."
9th Division
9th Division · Captain

Kensei Muguruma

Captain of the 9th Division · Visored

Bankai Tekken Tachikaze

Tachikaze — a small combat knife that fires wind blades in Shikai. Bankai wraps his fists in a tornado of cutting wind, enabling devastating close-range combat. As a Visored, he can amplify this further with his Hollow mask, massively boosting his already brutal close-quarters power.

"Talk less. Hit more. That's all the strategy I need."
10th Division
10th Division · Captain

Tōshirō Hitsugaya

Captain of the 10th Division · Child Prodigy

Bankai Daiguren Hyōrinmaru

Hyōrinmaru — the most powerful ice-type Zanpakutō, controlling heaven and earth ice. In Bankai, giant ice dragon wings and armor form on his body. Each wing tip can freeze everything within a 5-mile radius. In his true completed form, he becomes an adult — his full power finally realized.

"I don't need to explain myself to anyone. My blade speaks clearly enough."
11th Division
11th Division · Captain

Kenpachi Zaraki

Captain of the 11th Division · The Strongest Shinigami

Bankai Nozarashi

Nozarashi — a massive cleaver that amplifies raw power beyond all limits. He deliberately wore an eyepatch suppressing his spiritual power, and bells in his hair to warn enemies — making every fight fair. In Bankai he transforms into a demonic berserker form that obliterates anything in its path.

"The only thing I care about is the fight. Everything else is just noise."
12th Division
12th Division · Captain

Mayuri Kurotsuchi

Captain of the 12th Division · Head of Research & Development

Bankai Konjiki Ashisogi Jizō

Ashisogi Jizō — a grotesque baby-faced caterpillar that breathes paralysis gas keyed to each opponent's specific biology. Mayuri can modify his own body in real-time during combat and has pre-prepared solutions for thousands of abilities. He treats every opponent as an experiment.

"Science advances through experimentation — and you are today's most interesting specimen."
13th Division
13th Division · Captain

Jūshirō Ukitake

Captain of the 13th Division · Oldest Active Captain

Zanpakutō Sōgyo no Kotowari

Sōgyo no Kotowari — twin blades that absorb any energy attack and redirect it back multiplied. Since childhood, Ukitake has housed the right hand of the Soul King within his body — the spirit Mimihagi — which he ultimately sacrifices in the final war to protect Soul Society.

"A soul that has known suffering is not weakened by it — it is made deeper."
