The Court Guard Squads

The Gotei 13 (護廷十三隊, Goteijūsantai) is the primary military force of Soul Society, first formed over a thousand years ago. Originally thirteen brutal warriors led by the bloodthirsty Yama-jii, the organization evolved into a disciplined army structured around 13 numbered divisions, each with a distinct purpose and culture.

A Captain's requirement is absolute mastery of Bankai — a feat achieved by fewer than 1 in 1,000 Shinigami. Lieutenants, officers, and seated members fill the ranks below. Together they patrol the flow of souls between the living world and Soul Society, purify Hollows, and defend the balance of the universe.
